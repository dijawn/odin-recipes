Pasta Fagioli

Description

The Italian term “pasta e fagoili” translates in English to pasta and beans. It is a hearty soup consisting of plenty of the two.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Bacon
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Heat oil over medium heat
  2. Add bacon, carrots, celery, onion, and garlic
  3. Cook until tender
  4. Add chicken broth
