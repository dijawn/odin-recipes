Focaccia Barese

Description

It's a mix of grana duro semolina flour, white flour and potato in the dough.We do ours naturally leavened and use copious amounts of olive oil when baking.

Ingredients

  • Semolina Flour
  • White Flour
  • Golden Yukon Potato
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Sourdough Starter
  • Roma Tomato

Steps

  1. Pour the flour into a small bowl and make a hole in the center
  2. Pour water in
  3. Mix until shaggy
  4. Stretch and fold the dough
  5. Bulk fermentation
  6. Shape the dough
  7. Bake at 450 for 6 hours
